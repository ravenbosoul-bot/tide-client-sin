Features

Everything you want, nothing you don't.

Built on Fabric. Shipped with Sodium. Managed by a launcher that respects your time.

FPS-first defaults

Sodium, Lithium, FerriteCore, EntityCulling, Dynamic FPS, ModernFix — all auto-fetched from Modrinth for your exact MC version. No manual jar-hunting.

1-click Modrinth

Search Modrinth from inside the launcher. One click installs the correct Fabric build into your active profile. No browser, no downloads folder.

Per-version profiles

Swap between 1.21.10 and 1.21.11 and your mods swap with you. No more "incompatible mod" crashes from stale jars.

Built-in modules

FPS HUD, CPS, Keystrokes, Zoom, FreeCam, FullBright, Coordinates, Custom Crosshair — all toggle-able in-game. No external resource packs needed.

Microsoft auth

Sign in with your Mojang/Microsoft account via the official device-code flow. Offline mode available for single-player.

Open source

Launcher in Rust + Tauri, mod in Java + Fabric. Audit every line. Fork it. Build your own. MIT licensed.

In-game modules

18 modules. Zero config required.

Everything toggles from the in-game click-GUI. Settings persist per-version.
